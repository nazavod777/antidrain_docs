# Affiliate Link

The `/affiliate` page helps create a link with your EVM payout address for possible fee sharing.

## How to Create a Link

1. Open `/affiliate`.
2. Enter your EVM payout address.
3. Copy the link.
4. Share it with the user.

## How It Works

When a user opens the site with your link, your address can be saved locally as an inviter tag.

If the selected paid flow supports fee sharing, this address may receive part of the fee.

## Important

An affiliate link does not change rescue safety.

If the affiliate address cannot receive a native payout, execution should stay safe, but payout may use fallback logic.

## What to Check

Check that:

- the address is correct;
- the full link was copied;
- the user opened your link;
- the flow actually supports fee sharing.
