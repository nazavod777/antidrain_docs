AntiDrain Docs

AntiDrain Docs

AntiDrain is a browser-based EVM rescue workspace. It helps you prepare a donor wallet, choose a rescue action, simulate the transaction, fund execution, and send the reviewed transaction flow.

Choose Your Language

  • Русская документация
  • English documentation

Important

AntiDrain does not protect wallets automatically and does not monitor your wallet in the background. You choose every action, review it, and send it yourself.

Use small test amounts first. Always check addresses, network, simulation result, donor funding, and final transaction details before sending.
